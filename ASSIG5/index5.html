Switzerland

Switzerland is a small country in Europe. Capital — Bern, largest city — Zurich. Famous for the Alps, watches, chocolate, and neutrality.

England

England is part of the United Kingdom. Its capital is London. England is known for history, culture, football, and famous landmarks like Big Ben and Stonehenge.

France

France is a country in Western Europe. Its capital is Paris. France is famous for the Eiffel Tower, cuisine, fashion, wine, and art.
